Can I upload 18+ or Adult content?
No. SnapVector strictly prohibits the upload of pornography, nudity, gore, or any sexually explicit material. Accounts found violating this rule will be permanently banned and their IP addresses blocked.
How long do my images last?

SnapVector is a temporary host. All images are automatically deleted on the 1st of the next month.

  • Images uploaded in January are deleted on Feb 1st.
  • Images show a red border/glow 3 days before expiration.
I found an image that violates my Copyright. What do I do?
We take intellectual property rights seriously. If you find an image that belongs to you and was uploaded without permission, please contact the site administrator for a DMCA removal request. The image will be removed immediately.
Is my IP address tracked?
We do not store your actual IP address. We convert it into a cryptographic hash (SHA-256). This allows us to ban spammers without keeping a record of your real location or identity.
How do Guest Accounts work?
Guest accounts are anonymous and use randomly generated credentials. Warning: You cannot change the username or password of a guest account. If you log out or clear cookies without saving the credentials, the account is lost forever.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can delete individual images from your dashboard. Additionally, you can visit the Account settings and use the "Danger Zone" to permanently delete your account and all associated data.
